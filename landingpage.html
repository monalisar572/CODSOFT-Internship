About Us


Google is a global technology company that specializes in internet-related services and products

Founded in September 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin while they were Ph.D. students at Stanford University,

Google has since become one of the world's most recognized and influential technology companies.

The company's mission is to "organize the world's information and make it universally accessible and useful."

Key Information:

Founders: Larry Page and Sergey Brin

Incorporation Date: September 4, 1998

Headquarters: Mountain View, California, USA

CEO: Sundar Pichai

Parent Company: Alphabet Inc. (Google's parent company)

Employees: Google employs thousands of people globally, working in various locations around the world.

"Google's mission is to organize the world's information and make it universally accessible and useful." Google's "About" section showcases its commitment to innovation, user-centric products and services, and its influence on the digital landscape. The company's core principles revolve around making information accessible and improving the lives of people worldwide through technology.
